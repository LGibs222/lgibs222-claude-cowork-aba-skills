# Lessons

Read in order. Each is 5–10 minutes. After Lesson 3 you should be able to write a usable first skill.

| # | Lesson | What it covers |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | [What is a skill?](01-what-are-skills) | The concept, how it differs from a regular prompt, why it matters for repetitive consultation work |
| 2 | [Anatomy of a skill file](02-anatomy-of-a-skill) | Frontmatter, body, the `name` and `description` fields that make Claude pick the right skill |
| 3 | [Writing skills that actually fire](03-writing-effective-skills) | Trigger language, what to include, what to leave out, common mistakes |
| 4 | [ABA-specific patterns and recipes](04-aba-recipes) | Templates for FBAs, BIPs, IEP goals, parent updates, teacher coaching |
| 5 | [Installing and sharing skills](05-installing-skills) | Where files live, how to test, how to share with a team |
